Affiliate Disclosure

We believe in full transparency. This page explains exactly how Well Rested Skincare makes money, how that affects our editorial content, and what you can always count on from us.

Last Updated: March 1, 2026 · Well Rested Skincare, LLC

FTC Compliance

Our FTC Disclosure

Plain English: Some links on this website are affiliate links. If you click one and make a purchase, Well Rested Skincare, LLC may earn a commission — at no additional cost to you. We only recommend products and brands we genuinely believe in.

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials (16 CFR Part 255), Well Rested Skincare, LLC discloses that this website contains affiliate links and that we may receive compensation when you click on links to products and make a purchase.

This disclosure applies to all pages, posts, brand listings, product reviews, and editorial content published on wellrestedskincare.com.

Affiliate Links Explained

What is an affiliate link?

An affiliate link is a specially tracked URL that allows a brand or retailer to identify that a visitor came from our website. When you click an affiliate link on Well Rested Skincare and complete a purchase, we receive a small commission as compensation for the referral.

This commission costs you nothing. You pay exactly the same price whether you arrive at a brand's website through our link or by typing the URL directly into your browser. The commission is paid by the brand, not by you.

Affiliate links on this site will typically be associated with "Shop Now," "View Brand," or similar call-to-action buttons. Where affiliate relationships exist, they will be noted clearly in the content.

Editorial Independence

How this affects our recommendations

We take editorial independence seriously. Our affiliate relationships do not influence which brands we feature, how we review them, or what scores we assign. The following principles govern every piece of content on this site:

✓
Brands are reviewed on merit, not commercial relationship. We apply the same vetting criteria to every brand in our directory — affiliate partner or not. A brand with an affiliate deal does not receive a more favorable review.
✓
We feature brands we don't have affiliate deals with. Many brands in our directory are listed because they deserve to be there, even if no commercial relationship exists. Our goal is to be the most useful resource for our readers — not the most profitable.
✓
Negative findings are published. If a brand has weaknesses — in value, accessibility, clinical evidence, or real-world results — we say so. Our WRS Score reflects honest assessment, and we will not inflate a score to protect a commercial relationship.
✓
Affiliate status is always disclosed. Wherever an affiliate relationship exists, we clearly label it. You will never be sent to a brand's website through a tracked link without being told that's what is happening.
✓
We recommend better alternatives when they exist. If a more affordable, more effective, or more accessible product exists in the same category, we tell you — even if that product doesn't carry an affiliate commission for us.
