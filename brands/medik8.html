Katherine's Take

Why I Use Medik8

Medik8 is the brand that changed how I think about retinoids. I couldn't tolerate tretinoin — the irritation, the peeling, the weeks of adjustment that never quite resolved. Crystal Retinal was the first retinoid I could use consistently without my skin retaliating, and consistent use is the only thing that actually matters with any retinoid.

Retinaldehyde sits one conversion step from retinoic acid — your skin converts it directly, which is why it works faster than retinol and delivers results that are genuinely measurable, not theoretical. Crystal Retinal 1 is where I started. I'm now using Crystal Retinal 6. The graduated range means you can build tolerance intelligently rather than being handed a fixed prescription dose and told to manage.

The CSA philosophy — Vitamin C in the morning, SPF always, Vitamin A at night — is the simplest framework I've found for explaining an effective anti-aging routine. Everything Medik8 makes is built around it. Their Liquid Peptides and C-Tetra Serum are also in my regular rotation. This is a brand I trust, which I can't say about many.
