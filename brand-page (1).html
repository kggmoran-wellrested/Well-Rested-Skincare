Brand Overview

The antioxidant pioneers

SkinCeuticals was founded on a single scientific breakthrough: the discovery that antioxidants, when formulated at the correct pH and concentration, could measurably prevent and reverse free radical damage to the skin. That discovery — made by Dr. Sheldon Pinnell at Duke University — became the foundation for every product in the line.

Today, SkinCeuticals occupies a unique space in skincare: sold through dermatologists and licensed professionals rather than department stores, every formula undergoes rigorous clinical testing before it reaches shelves. The brand doesn't chase trends — it sets them.
